# ProblemSolverAI

ProblemSolverAI is a patent-pending AI system designed to analyze real-world problems from images and provide step-by-step repair or troubleshooting solutions.

## Concept

Many people encounter problems with objects in the real world but do not know how to diagnose or fix them.

Examples include:

• damaged materials (wood, drywall, appliances)  
• broken household items  
• mechanical or equipment issues  
• DIY repair situations  

ProblemSolverAI aims to allow a user to simply:

1. 1. Take a photo or short video of the problem
2. Upload the image
3. Receive AI-generated diagnostic insight
4. Follow step-by-step repair instructions

## Technology Direction

The system may involve:

• computer vision models  
• object recognition  
• defect detection  
• large language models for instruction generation  
• multimodal AI pipelines combining vision + reasoning

## Vision

The long-term goal is to create an AI assistant capable of understanding real-world problems visually and guiding users through practical solutions.

This could eventually expand into areas such as:

• home repair  
• equipment troubleshooting  
• automotive diagnostics  
• industrial maintenance  
• accessibility tools for non-experts
